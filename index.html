The Team

Namra Zulfiqar

Namra has participated in a variety of clubs since freshman year and using the leadership she's obtained from positions like Sophomore Caucus Vice President, Stuy ink. Secretary, and Belly Crew Asst. Director, she hopes to plan an amazing Junior Year from another dance, a better JProm, the creation of AP Art History, and bigger and better college trips.

Enver Ramadani

As Director of Event Planning for PACT and Director of Contemporary Dance for SING!, both his freshman and sophomore year, Enver has had a variety of directorial and leadership experiences. Through choreographing and organizing, Enver has learned how to make last minute changes and detailed plans and a variety of other skills, all of which come in handy when in a leadership position. As a member of the boys gymnastics team and a Big Sib, Enver has also gained experiences helpful when working with groups of people. As your Junior Caucus Vice President, Enver will work with your President to make sure all of your needs are fulfilled and more.
